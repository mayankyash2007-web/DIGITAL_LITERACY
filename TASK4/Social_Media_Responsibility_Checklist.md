# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

This checklist highlights responsible social media practices for college students to ensure safe, respectful, and positive online behavior.

---

## ✅ Do’s

- 🔐 Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts  
- 🔒 Keep your profile privacy settings updated  
- 🧠 Think before posting any content online  
- 🌱 Share useful, informative, and positive content  
- 🤝 Respect others and communicate politely  

---

## ❌ Don’ts

- 🚫 Don’t share personal information (address, phone number, etc.)  
- ⚠️ Don’t post offensive, abusive, or harmful content  
- 🔗 Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links  
- ❌ Don’t spread fake news or unverified information  
- 📍 Don’t overshare your location or daily activities  

---

## 📌 Conclusion

Following these simple do’s and don’ts can help students maintain a safe digital presence, protect their privacy, and build a positive online identity.
